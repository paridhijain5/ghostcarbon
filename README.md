# 🌍 GhostCarbon: Parallel Earth

**Replay your life. Reduce what you never saw.**

GhostCarbon is an AI-powered storytelling experience that helps people understand, track, and reduce their carbon footprint through personalized insights and alternate low-carbon realities.

Instead of showing boring numbers and dashboards, GhostCarbon creates two worlds:

- 🌍 **Earth A** – Your actual lifestyle
- ✨ **Earth B** – A parallel low-carbon version of your life

Users can replay their day, discover hidden emissions, and explore how small changes can create a better future.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🔄 Replay Today
Compare your actual day with a low-carbon alternative and visualize the impact of simple changes.

### 👻 Invisible Carbon Shadow
Reveal hidden emissions from daily activities such as transport, food delivery, streaming, and AI usage.

### 🤖 Ghost Twin
Simulate the long-term impact of better habits and estimate yearly carbon and money savings.

### 🌎 Parallel Earth
Explore two possible futures and understand how lifestyle choices affect the planet over time.

### 📖 Weekly Story
A Spotify Wrapped-inspired summary of your carbon habits presented as an engaging story instead of charts.

### 💬 Ghost Messages
Receive encouraging messages from Earth B, your cleaner and more sustainable parallel self.
